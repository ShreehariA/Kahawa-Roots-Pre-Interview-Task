## 1. What the Company Does

**Kahawa Roots** is a **coffee traceability and ethical supply chain company** that connects **Kenyan smallholder coffee farmers** directly with **UK cafes and roasters**. They source premium Arabica coffee from the **Mt. Elgon region** (Bungoma and surrounding areas in western Kenya) and ensure full traceability from farm to cup.

### Core Business Model
- **B2B wholesale** of traceable Kenyan coffee to UK-based cafes and roasters
- **SIC Code:** 46370 – *Wholesale of coffee, tea, cocoa and spices*
- Every bag of coffee can be traced back to the individual farmer who grew it
- Emphasis on fair compensation for farmers and transparent supply chain

### Coffee Sourced
- **Region:** Mt. Elgon, Bungoma, Western Kenya (altitude 1,400–2,200m)
- **Varieties:** SL28, SL34, Batian, Ruiru 11 (all Arabica)
- **Processing:** Washed (wet) method
- **Grading:** AA (premium), AB, PB
- **Harvest seasons:** Main: Oct–Dec, Secondary: Mar–May
- **Flavour profile:** Bright acidity, citrus/berry notes, medium-to-full body, floral undertones
- Beans are hand-picked by smallholder farmers on 0.5–2 hectare plots
- Farmers organized into cooperative societies (e.g., Bukusu Cooperative Society)
